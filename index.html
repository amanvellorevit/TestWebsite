About me

Personal Projects

Irrigation System

I have developed an Irrigation system for my garden consisting of around 40 plants. Automation is my hobby, this system saves a lot of my time and water and keep my plants happy.

IoT Automation

With the help of ESP8266, a chip microcontroller, I have implemented a switch connection from my smart phone to this wifi enabled microcontroller. In parallel there is a relay connecting the microcontroller which converts 5v microcontroller switch to handle 220v rating equipment. One microchip can be used to connect 5 electrical equipment. I can switch on my AC or Geyser remotely 10 minutes prior to reaching home from work.

Irrigation with IoT

I have integrated a low power water pump with the irrigation system which was earlier connected to the tap. This motor is integrated with the ESP8266 microcontroller, the switch of which I can directly control from my smartphone. With the help of this system I am able to water my Garden remotely from anywhere in the world through internet connection. This avoid wastage of water yet facilitating sufficient water even when I am on a long vacation or on a business trip.

Free power

This mini project is based on the principle of Electromagnetic induction. When a rotating magnetic field( change in the magnetic flux) is brought in close proximity with the coil of copper wires, a current is induced in the coil. This current can be amplified for external usage. With the help of this mini project I was able to at least glow an led light. Its an interesting project, my next milestone is to use this power in home appliances.
